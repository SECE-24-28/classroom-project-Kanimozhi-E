ABOUT ME

I’m Kanimozhi, a passionate soul from the IT department, driven not just by technology but by the thrill of understanding things deeply. I love exploring concepts until they feel alive—until learning turns into a beautiful form of listening.

Outside the world of engineering, I’m a storyteller at heart. I enjoy listening to stories, writing poems, and capturing the charm of nature through photographs. The sky, leaves, rains, and sunsets often feel like silent teachers to me.

My journey into engineering began with fascination—how a simple innovation can transform the life of an ordinary person. That spark still pushes me to learn, explore, and create.

I’m equally drawn to the world of psychology, where understanding a person’s mind feels as intriguing as reading a good story. Add to that my love for science and the history behind it, and I become a curious traveler across ideas—past, present, and future.

What I love the most is deep learning—the moment when a topic pulls me in so much that I stop studying and start listening, as if the concept itself is speaking to me.
